# Privacy Policy

**Mental Maths Marathon**  
Last updated: July 11, 2026

## Data Collection

Mental Maths Marathon does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The app operates entirely offline with no network access.

### What the app stores locally (on your device only):
- **Game statistics** (scores, streaks, accuracy) — stored via SharedPreferences
- **Achievements & level progress** — stored locally
- **App preferences** (sound, music, dark mode, game settings) — stored locally

### No data is:
- Sent to any server
- Shared with third parties
- Used for analytics or advertising
- Linked to your identity

## Permissions

- **Notifications** — Used only for optional daily practice reminders. No data is transmitted.

## Third-Party Services

This app uses no third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

## Changes

If this policy changes, the "Last updated" date will be revised.
